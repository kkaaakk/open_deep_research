# Tesla China Regulatory Compliance — Battery & Safety

## Key Regulations

### GB38031-2025 "Electric Vehicles Traction Battery Safety Requirements"
- **Effective**: 2026-07-01
- **Scope**: All EVs sold in China, including imported models
- **Critical Change**: Battery pack must provide ≥5 minutes warning BEFORE thermal runaway causes fire/explosion (prior standard required warning only after fire started)
- **Test Protocols**: New thermal propagation test; enhanced overcharge, over-discharge, short-circuit, and crush tests
- **Tesla Compliance Status**: All models produced after 2026-01-01 validated. Certificates on file with MIIT.

### MIIT "Three-Fire Investigation" Mechanism (2026)
- Three or more fire incidents on same model → MIIT may revoke vehicle type approval (product announcement)
- Applies retroactively to all vehicles currently in service
- Investigation timeline: MIIT notifies manufacturer → 30 days to submit root cause analysis → MIIT decides on enforcement action

### SAMR Recall Regulations (2020 Edition, updated 2024)
- Defect investigations can be initiated by SAMR sua sponte (without consumer complaint threshold)
- OTA updates that "correct defects" must be reported to SAMR as recalls within 5 business days
- Failure to report an OTA recall → administrative penalties up to 3% of prior year revenue

### Consumer Rights Law — E-Commerce & Auto Specific Provisions
- "Three Guarantees" (三包) for NEVs effective 2022-01-01:
  - 7 days: Return/refund for major safety defects
  - 60 days / 3,000km: Replacement for steering, braking, powertrain failures
  - Battery capacity degradation warranty: minimum 70% within warranty period
- 12315 platform response requirement: 7 business days
